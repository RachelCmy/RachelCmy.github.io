Mengyu (Rachel) Chu · 楚梦渝

Biography

I am a Lise Meitner Postdoctoral Research Fellow at Max Planck Institute for Informatics since 2020, supervised by Prof. Dr. Christian Theobalt and Dr. Rhaleb Zayer, in group D6 (Visual Computing and Artificial Intelligence) and D4 (Computer Graphics). Previously, I was as a Ph.D. student in Prof. Nils Thuerey’s group, at Technical University of Munich, from 2014 to 2020. I received my M.Eng. degree from Zhejiang University, China in March 2014.

Research Interest

I'm interested in Physics-Enhanced Deep Learning. Combining deep-learning-based algorithms and physical simulations, the research aims to improve the flexibility of simulations and to enhance the accuracy and generalizability of deep learning models.
